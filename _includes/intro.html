UTA's first 24 hour hackathon

HackUTA is University of Texas at Arlington's first ever 24 hour hackathon, hosted by UTA students. The event will take place in Nedderman Hall on the weekend of September 30th and October 1st. HackUTA is more than just software, it's getting together to innovate, work with others and create something cool in 24 hours.

What's a hack? Projects range from VR Flappy Bird, to IOT sticker vending machines, to an app that uses machine learning to create a song. This all sounds fancy but even a simple website to help fight hunger is considered a hack!
