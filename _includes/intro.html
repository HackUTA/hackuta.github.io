UTA's first 24 hour hackathon

HackUTA is University of Texas at Arlingtons's first ever 24 hour hackathon, hosted by Mobi. The event will take place in Nedderman Hall on April 15th - 16th. HackUTA is more than just software, it's getting together to innovate, work with others and create something cool in 24 hours.

What's a hack? Projects range from VR Flappy Bird, to IOT sticker vending machines, to an app that uses machine learning to create a song. This all sounds fancy but even a simple website to help fight hunger is considered a hack!
